milk-and-water is a pretty awesome site envisioned and developed by a pair of undergrad graphic designers at the Maryland Institute College of Art. Launching April 29, 2016, we're holding an exhibition IRL that you're welcome to stop by and participate in, should you be in the Baltimore area.

So what is this, you ask?

milk-and-water is a collaborative pop-up site, merging forces between coders and non-coders to enjoy all of the small-scale code inanities the world wide web has to offer. Mostly, we're about making cool things, so we've left this site here with some pretty rad throwbacks to the internet circa 2005.

We hope you'll take the opportunity to barter with us, or just contribute your own content to keep things going. We use GitHub, so do with that what you will. Steal-and-runs are also fair game, all of our pages are here for you to take.
